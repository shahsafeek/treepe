Building meaningful partnerships in travel

Treepe is a digital partnerships platform that brings together trusted travel brands and over 300,000 dedicated influencers and content creators worldwide.

Creators

Creators recommend the travel brands they love to their audience and get rewarded for their efforts.

Travelers

Travelers book their perfect trips at the best price and explore fascinating places, both near and far.

Travel Brands

Travel brands connect with influencers and content creators to extend their reach and increase sales.

Experience

People are at the heart of everything we do. To give our partners the edge and help them thrive, we share our unique experience and expertise in the travel niche. This enables our partners to create top-quality content that drives growth and sales.

Freedom

Freedom is our core value. So, we empower every partner, no matter how big their audience is, to monetize their content and turn the money earned into the freedom to travel and choose their path in life.

Honesty

We believe that honesty is the best policy and act with honesty at all times, partnering with trusted brands and creators only, nipping fraud-related activities in the bud, and providing transparent data on how brands and partners perform.

Technology

Technology is in our DNA. To help brands and creators succeed, we provide cutting-edge technological solutions, including the all-in-one easy-to-use dashboard, a powerful suite of promo and automation tools, granular reports and more.

Reward

We are obsessed with travel and believe that it’s possible to live off your passions. So, as a company, we do our best to help both travel brands and creators monetize their love for travel and thrive.
